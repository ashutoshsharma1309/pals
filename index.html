05_faq

FAQ

Who can participate?

Students from B.E., M.Tech, MBA, MCA, and related academic programs are welcome to participate.

Do I need a team?

Teams will be formed randomly before the hackathon to encourage collaboration and interaction among participants.

Is there a registration fee?

No. Registration for the hackathon is free.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger, ID card, and any hardware parts your idea needs.

Will there be food?

Refreshments will be provided during the workshop.

Do I need prior experience?

No. The workshop is designed to help participants learn before they build.
